Understanding Neural Networks

Neural networks are the foundation of many AI systems today. They mimic how the human brain processes information, using interconnected layers of nodes called "neurons". With enough training data, they can recognize patterns, classify images, or even generate text.

This beginner-friendly guide will give you a clear picture of how inputs flow through layers, how weights are adjusted during training, and why neural networks are so powerful for tasks like vision and language processing.
