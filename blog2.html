Ethical Considerations in AI Development

Artificial Intelligence is shaping our future, but with great power comes great responsibility. Ethical AI means building systems that are fair, transparent, and accountable.

Developers must consider issues such as bias in data, privacy of users, and the impact of automation on jobs and society. Addressing these challenges ensures AI benefits everyone, not just a few.

By keeping ethics at the core of AI development, we can create technology that improves lives while respecting human values.
